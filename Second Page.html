HOW TO MAKE A SANDWICH

  1. Get out two slices of bread
  2. Spread sauce(s) on either or both slices of bread
  3. Add meats to slice 1
  4. Add veggies on top of meats
  5. Place slice 2 on top
  6. Slice in half if desired
